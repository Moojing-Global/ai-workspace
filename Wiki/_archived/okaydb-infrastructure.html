What is OkayDB?

OkayDB is a comprehensive database and verification hub that stores all customer identity data captured during the eKYC journey and connects with third-party data sources for enhanced verification. It serves as the central repository for your identity verification operations, enabling you to not only store customer information securely but also enrich it with external data from credit bureaus, sanctions lists, watchlists, and other trusted sources.

Unlike simple storage solutions, OkayDB combines data warehousing with active verification capabilities. Through customizable API integrations, businesses can perform real-time checks against multiple databases simultaneously—verifying license expiry status, screening against PEP lists, checking credit scores, analyzing digital footprints, and more. This transforms your eKYC process from a simple identity capture system into a comprehensive risk assessment platform.

What Problems Does It Solve?

Fragmented verification data creates operational chaos

Without a centralized database, customer verification data becomes scattered across multiple systems, departments, and vendors. Teams waste hours searching for records, compliance auditors struggle to find documentation, and customer service cannot quickly access verification history. This fragmentation leads to duplicated efforts, inconsistent decision-making, and increased operational costs. OkayDB consolidates all verification data in one secure location, providing instant access to complete customer profiles.

Manual third-party checks are slow and error-prone

Verifying customers against sanctions lists, PEP databases, credit bureaus, and other external sources traditionally requires manual searches across multiple platforms. Compliance officers spend days copying data between systems, increasing the risk of human error and creating bottlenecks during customer onboarding. A single missed check can result in regulatory violations and substantial fines. OkayDB automates these checks through API integrations, delivering results in seconds with perfect accuracy.

Ongoing due diligence is nearly impossible to maintain

Regulations require continuous monitoring of customers—PEP status changes, new sanctions list additions, updated credit scores—but manually re-verifying thousands of existing customers is impractical. Most businesses only verify customers once during onboarding, leaving them vulnerable to evolving risks. OkayDB enables automated periodic re-verification, flagging customers whose risk profiles have changed and ensuring continuous compliance.

Data silos prevent fraud detection and risk assessment

When identity data, verification results, transaction history, and third-party checks exist in separate systems, businesses cannot detect patterns that indicate fraud. A customer might pass individual checks but exhibit suspicious combinations—new high-risk device paired with recently added sanctions list entry, for example. OkayDB centralizes all data points, enabling holistic risk assessment and advanced fraud detection that individual checks would miss.

How Businesses Use It

Digital Banking and Fintech

Banks and fintech companies integrate OkayDB into their customer lifecycle management systems to create comprehensive customer profiles. During account opening, OkayDB automatically stores captured ID data, biometric information, and verification results while simultaneously checking the customer against sanctions lists, PEP databases, and credit bureaus. This enables same-day account approval with complete regulatory compliance. For existing customers, OkayDB performs scheduled re-verification, flagging accounts that require enhanced due diligence when risk indicators change.

Cryptocurrency and Investment Platforms

Crypto exchanges and investment platforms use OkayDB to manage high-volume verification requirements while maintaining strict AML compliance. The platform stores detailed customer profiles and connects with blockchain analytics providers, sanctions screening services, and financial crime databases. When a user attempts a high-value transaction, OkayDB provides instant access to complete verification history and current risk scores, enabling real-time transaction decisions without manual review.

E-Commerce and Marketplace Platforms

Online marketplaces leverage OkayDB to verify merchant identities and assess seller risk. The database stores verification records for thousands of sellers while integrating with business registration databases, credit agencies, and fraud intelligence networks. This enables platforms to automatically approve low-risk sellers instantly while flagging high-risk applications for enhanced verification, protecting buyers and maintaining marketplace integrity.

Telecommunications Providers

Telecom operators use OkayDB to comply with SIM registration regulations while preventing fraud. At retail locations and through digital channels, customer identity data flows into OkayDB along with device information and third-party verification results. The system checks government watchlists, verifies address information against utility databases, and stores complete audit trails for regulatory reporting. This enables instant SIM activation while maintaining full compliance with local regulations.

Healthcare and Insurance

Healthcare providers and insurers utilize OkayDB to verify patient and policyholder identities while protecting sensitive medical data. The platform integrates with national health databases, insurance claim history systems, and medical licensing verification services. This prevents medical identity fraud, reduces fraudulent insurance claims, and ensures only authorized individuals access medical records and services.
